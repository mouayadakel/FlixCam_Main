# User Guide – Client Portal (Phase 9.1)

## Access

- Log in at `/login`; after login you can open the portal at `/portal/dashboard` or via “حسابي” / “بوابة العميل”.

## Dashboard & Bookings

- **لوحة التحكم:** Overview and quick links.
- **حجوزاتي:** List of your bookings. Click a booking to see details.

## Booking Detail Actions

- **طلب تعديل:** Request a change (dates, equipment, etc.). Describe the change and submit; support will review.
- **طلب تمديد:** Request a new end date. Enter the new end date and reason; price difference will be communicated after approval.
- **إلغاء الحجز:** Cancel the booking. Allowed only within policy (e.g. at least 48 hours before start for confirmed bookings). Optional reason.
- **الإبلاغ عن ضرر:** Report damage (for active or returned bookings). Select equipment (if applicable), type, severity, description, and estimated cost.

## Profile & Documents

- **الملف الشخصي:** Update your name and phone. Email is read-only. 2FA will be available later.
- **المستندات:** Hub for contracts and invoices with links to full lists.
- **العقود / الفواتير:** View and sign contracts; view and download invoices.

## Support

- Use the WhatsApp button (pre-filled message) or contact support for any issue.
